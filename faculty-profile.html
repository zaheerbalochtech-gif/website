Biography

Dr. Haneef holds a PhD in Computer Science with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence and Data Mining. With over 10 years of teaching and research experience, he has contributed significantly to the field of Machine Learning applied to local linguistic data. He is currently leading the "Digital Balochistan" research initiative at UoT, aiming to digitize historical manuscripts using OCR technology.
