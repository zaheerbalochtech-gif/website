Undergraduate Program

BS Computer Science

A rigorous academic journey designed to foster critical thinking, technical expertise, and leadership skills for the modern professional landscape.

Program Overview

The BS Computer Science program focuses on the theoretical and practical aspects of computing. It prepares students for careers in software development, data science, and system architecture. The curriculum is designed to meet international standards (ACM/IEEE) and includes a mix of core computing courses, supporting sciences, and electives that allow students to specialize in areas like AI, Networks, or Web Engineering.

Students will engage in hands-on projects, lab work, and a final year capstone project that solves real-world problems. Our faculty members, who are experts in their fields, provide mentorship and guidance throughout the academic journey.

Program Objectives

  • To provide a solid foundation in computer science theory and practice.
  • To develop problem-solving skills and the ability to design efficient software solutions.
  • To prepare students for leadership roles in the technology sector and academia.

Career Opportunities

Software Engineer Data Scientist System Analyst Network Administrator Web Developer AI Specialist

Eligibility

  • Intermediate (HSSC) Pre-Engineering / ICS with at least 50% marks or equivalent qualifications.
  • Successful clearance of the University Entry Test.
  • Candidates with A-Levels must provide an equivalence certificate from IBCC.
  • Age limit: 24 years at time of admission.
